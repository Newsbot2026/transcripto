Frequently Asked Questions

Is Transcripto free to use?
Yes! You get 2 free transcript extractions without signing up. After that, upgrade to our Basic or Pro plan for unlimited access.
Which videos are supported?
All public YouTube videos that have captions or auto-generated subtitles are supported. This includes Hindi, English and most major languages.
Do I need to create an account?
No account needed for free trials! Just paste the link and get your transcript instantly. Account is only needed for paid plans.
How accurate are the transcripts?
We use YouTube's own captions data — same source that powers YouTube's CC feature. Accuracy depends on the video's original captions quality.
Can I download the transcript?
Yes! Every transcript can be downloaded as a .txt file or copied to clipboard with one click — completely free.
What happens after my free trials are used?
After 2 free extractions, you can subscribe to our Pro plan (₹349/month) for unlimited access. Payment is 100% secure via Razorpay.
